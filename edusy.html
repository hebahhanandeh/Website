Educational system

1)This system shall be called (the System of Certification of Academic Certificates of 1992) and it shall be enforced from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.

2)The following words and expressions, wherever mentioned in this system, shall have the meanings assigned to them below, unless the context indicates otherwise:

3)The Ministry: The Ministry of Education.

4)The Minister: The Minister of Education.

5)The Directorate: The Directorate of Education in the governorate or the district.

6)General Certificate: General Secondary School Certificate and any certificate granted or granted by the Ministry as a result of a general examination.

7)Academic Certificate: The scientific certificate and any document that shows the scientific level of its owner and is issued by educational institutions of various levels inside and outside the Kingdom.

 

8)Academic certificates issued by educational institutions in the Kingdom defined by the education law in force according to the following rules:

A - The public certificate is signed by the minister or his authorized representative, and does not require approval.

B - The Ministry or the competent directorate approves a copy of the public certificate and copies of the transcript of marks for that certificate with the phrase (a true copy).

C - The Ministry approves the authenticity of the signature and the official stamp affixed to the scientific certificate issued by the educational institution not affiliated with the Ministry, after validating the authenticity of the information in that certificate from the authority that issued it.

D- The directorate approves the authenticity of the signature and official seal affixed to the scientific certificate issued by private and foreign schools.

9)The approval of the scientific certificate and its copies on the validity of the signature of the person authorized in the ministry or directorate of the signature and the seal approved for each of them, as the case may be.

 

10) Scientific certificates issued by educational institutions outside the Kingdom are certified according to the following rules:

A- The Jordanian cultural advisor in the country in which the certificate was issued certifies the authenticity of the signatures and seals approved by the official authorities responsible for education in that country, and the ministry or directorate certifies the authenticity of the signature and stamp of the Jordanian cultural advisor.

B - In the absence of a Jordanian cultural advisor in any country in which the Jordanian diplomatic mission certifies the validity of the signature of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of that country and its stamp, provided that the certificate is certified by the official authorities responsible for education in it, and the Jordanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs authenticates the validity of the signature of the Jordanian diplomatic mission and its seal, and the Ministry approves Or the directorate signing the Jordanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs and its seal.

C- If the scientific certificate cannot be certified according to the provisions of this Bylaw in any case, it can be certified by the Ministry after verification of its authenticity in the manner it deems appropriate.

 

11) In cases not provided for in this system, it is referred to the Ministry of Education Committee for decision.

 

12)The Minister shall issue the necessary instructions to implement the provisions of this system, including the following:

A- Procedures for certifying academic certificates and copies thereof from the competent authorities and directorates in the Ministry and any other school certificate.

B- Procedures related to preparing and maintaining scales of marks that educational institutions have to follow.

13)The Scientific Certification System No. -29- of 1983 is repealed.

Ministry of Education (Jordan)
