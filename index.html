Minister

Tayseer Al-Nuaimi

Minister

Tayseer Manzil Al-Nahar Al-Nuaimi, (1955, Irbid) Jordanian university lecturer and politician, since (7 November 2019) has held the position of Minister of Education in Jordan. QualificationsThe most prominent educational qualifications PhD in Educational Psychology - University of Pittsburgh / USA 1986 Master of Education (psychological guidance counseling) - Yarmouk University 1981 Bachelor of Arts - University of Jordan 1977

Muhammad Al-Thunaibat

Minister

Muhammad Al-Thunaibat (born in 1950 in Karak) is a Jordanian politician who held the position of Deputy Prime Minister in addition to the position of Minister of Education from October 2012 until January 14, 2017 in the governments of Abdullah Al-Nsour and Hani Al-Mulqi. Early in 2003, when he was one of the ministerial staff and held the position of Minister of Administrative Development and Minister of Environment in the government of Ali Abu Ragheb, he suffered an unfortunate traffic accident in the Qatraneh area in southern Jordan, which led to the death of his wife.

Azmi Mohafaza

Minister

Azmi Mohafaza was the Minister of Education and the Jordanian Minister of Higher Education and Scientific Research in the government of Omar Razzaz until 1 November 2018. He is a former President of the University of Jordan. educational qualifications Bachelor degree in human medicine from Damascus University in 1977. PhD in Basic Medical Sciences / Microbiology and Immunology from the American University of Beirut in 1984. Fellowship in virology from Yale University in 1988.

Bassam Al-Talhouni

Minister

Bassam Al-Talhouni is a Jordanian lawyer, politician, and academic who held the position of Minister of Industry and Minister of Justice. Education He holds a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Law from the University of Jordan. In 1997 he received his PhD in Law from the University of Edinburgh
